Back then people knew what true magic was, because it was everywhere. Magic is strongest when it comes from the heart. And so, my student, let me share with you one of the spells of the ancient times, when magic was strong, to defeat enemies and banish ghosts.

A thing happens when mortals lose touch with reality. Their sould twist, they linger, terrifying and dangerous beings of melevolence who can kill with a touch, a look, or a wail. But master this ritual and you will be able to defeat them handily.

It has not complex runes, no arcane ingredients. Instead you must do only one thing, and do it well: Love another with all your heart. It is both the easiest and hardest spell to cast in all the world.
