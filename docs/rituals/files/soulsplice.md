# A Spell For The Binding of Souls

## Overview

My Daughter. Every wytch hath her own way of becoming the best wytch which she can be. Your feet need not follow the path which I have walked, but if you choose to follow in mine footsteps than I hope to have made your travel easier that you may travel farther than I ever have. To that end, I leave with you this spell. As with all Magick, its drawbacks will outweigh its uses unless applied with utmost care. I give to you three warnings, with the hope that you will think thrice befor you cast it. Firstly, NEVER use it unless you are sure of the kindliness of the spirit with which you wish to merge. Secondly, I hope I need not remind you that touching your soul to another is permanent, you cannot share that kind of space and walk away unaffected. And lastly, but most importantly, it is not a spell you can use twice. Holding one soul in you at a time is enough of a challenge, there is no sane reason to try for three.
